How is this calculated?

Your daily consumption is spread evenly across all 96 intervals (15 min each).

The battery charges during the cheapest intervals first, limited by its capacity and efficiency. During the most expensive intervals, stored energy covers your consumption instead of grid power.

Savings = (baseline grid cost) − (grid cost with battery). Charging cost is included in the battery scenario.
