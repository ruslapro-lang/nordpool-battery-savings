How is this calculated?

When you upload a CSV, the calculator averages your actual consumption across all days to build a typical 15-minute profile.

The battery charges during the cheapest intervals (limited by charger power). It discharges during the most expensive intervals where you actually have consumption — it only charges as much as it can usefully discharge.

Savings = baseline grid cost − grid cost with battery (charging cost included).
