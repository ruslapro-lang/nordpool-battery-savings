How the calculation works

With your CSV uploaded: each day's actual 15-minute consumption is matched to that day's real Nordpool prices from ENTSO-E. Savings are calculated day by day and summed.

Without CSV: a typical Lithuanian household evening-peak profile is used with today's prices.

Strategy: the battery charges during the cheapest available intervals (limited by charger power). It discharges during the most expensive intervals — but only when you actually have consumption at that time. The battery never charges more than it can usefully discharge.

Formula: Saving = Σ(consumption × grid price) − Σ((consumption − discharge + charge) × price). Charging cost is fully included.

Spot prices from ENTSO-E Transparency Platform (no VAT, no grid fees, no supplier margin). Your actual bill savings will differ — spot price contracts pass these savings through; fixed-rate contracts do not.
